Lead to Payout Journey Process

Refer a Lead

Refer a lead through the online platform, mobile app or through the call center

Login

ICICI representative collects documents and logs in the file

Sanction

Case gets decisioned and the customer is intimated

Disbursement

Property documents are collected and verified Loan is disbursed

Payout

You receive the referral fee based on the amount disbursed


Let us take you through the lead to payout journey at ICICI Bank. You journey starts with lead referral. You can refer a lead either of the 3 ways listed above.

Post referral your mapped counselor or Branch Sales Manager (BSM) will contact the customer and collect all the required documents from the customer. Once the documents are collected, they are submitted to our Central Processing Center for appraisal.

The customer receives the sanction letter based on his eligibility and can request for disbursement. On request of disbursement request from customer, your mapped counselor will do the required documentation and collect the property documents from the customer.

For the cases disbursed in any calendar month, the "payout" or referral fee amount will be computed and credited by 10th of next month
